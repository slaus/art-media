FAQ

Frequently asked Questions

HOW IMPORTANT IS TEXT CONTENT FOR MY WEBSITE?

Google makes search results based on textual content, which is a very small percentage compared to images (Google images). Content is still king and will remain so.

When you write text for your website, take the time to make it informative and of high quality. It will pay off.

IF I DECIDE TO CHOOSE YOU, HOW MANY KEYWORDS CAN I FOCUS ON FOR MY CAMPAIGN?

The number of keywords is not limited. You are buying a service or time that someone spends working on your website and campaign. If you choose one or more keywords, it's not the same as ten. The time of the campaign is expressed in working hours, we advise you to choose the optimal number of keywords that can have a big impact on a particular optimization period.

Sometimes it's better to focus on 2-3 keywords than to spend "energy" on a large number of them and not get the desired results.

DO YOU DO SEO IN A SPECIFIC COUNTRY OR DO YOU HAVE A WIDE COVERAGE?

Each market has its own search engine in addition to the global Google.com. Using proven methodologies, we can analyze the competition in any search engine and in any language.

Geographic location is not important and our clients are from all continents.

WILL MY WEBSITE RANK IN GOOGLE AND DO YOU PROVIDE A GUARANTEE?

In addition to you and your competitors, Google is constantly working to improve its position. Google changes its algorithm frequently, and search results are influenced by many factors.

The company that gives you a guarantee has no basis for it, because no one can predict exactly how much money the competition is investing and what scenarios are possible. The main advantage of SEO companies is their current results. It can be guaranteed that only methods that meet Google's conditions will be used, and both positive results and good positions are possible.

HOW CAN I TRACK SEO RESULTS AND SEE WHAT HAS BEEN DONE?

SEO results are very easy to monitor by changing your position in the search engine, increasing the number of queries/keywords, increasing the number of visits. Monthly reports from SEO specialists will clearly show all marketing activity.
